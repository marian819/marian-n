About Us

Nabs Savings & Loans is a Ghanaian company that was originally incorporated in 2020 as Nabs Investments; an investment consultancy firm. The company has had a smooth run till 2022 when its promoters decided to inject additional capital to meet the minimum capital requirement of GHC 5m for the establishment of Savings and Loans companies. As a fully capitalized Savings and Loans company, the Central Bank of Ghana; Bank of Ghana issued it with the final operating license in January 2023 to undertake the business of financial intermediation.
